About Us

G1 Path Lab is a pathology laboratory situated in Nanded city road, Dhayari Pune- 41, providing qualityclinicaldiagnostic services. It was established in December 2020 by Mr. Gundappa Khadake with Dr. Mayank Sharma sir and Dr. Laximan Karle sir as a routine Pathology and ClinicalDiagnostic Laboratory. We provide the services of home visits, online reporting, one-on-one discussion of reports with doctor along with facility for multiple diagnostic tests for routine as well as high end cancer diagnostics, all under one roof. We are committed towards delivering on-time, precise reports to patients through use ofadvanced technologyand latest instruments, with a quick turnaround time while maintaining a strict ethical code and sensitivitytowards ourwork environment. Our Central Lab is located at Nanded city Road, NearLokmat Office, Dhayari Pune - 411041 In addition we have collection centres in tie up with Physicians, Gynaecologists, Haematologists and Oncologists across Pune.
